The Story Behind ShortcutHub

Ashish Cherian

GitHub | LinkedIn

I am Ashish, and this page started from a very practical frustration: every day, I opened the same tools, but they were scattered across tabs, bookmarks, and history.

I wanted something personal and fast. Not another heavy dashboard, but a focused launchpad. So I planned ShortcutHub around one idea: fewer clicks, cleaner flow, better energy.

Problem: messy browser start experience.
Thought process: keep only what helps daily execution.
Solution: grouped shortcuts, powerful settings, rich backgrounds, and a creator-led style.
