Creator Story

Ashish Cherian

Builder of ShortcutHub

ShortcutHub started from a daily friction: opening the same websites repeatedly from scattered bookmarks and tabs. The vision was simple and practical, to build a clean, customizable launchpad that keeps focus high and clicks low. The project grew from a basic shortcuts board into a full personal start page with modern UI, grouped links, smart settings, media backgrounds, and creator-first personalization.

Idea to Solution

  • Idea: one hub for all essential daily websites.
  • Problem: bookmarks are cluttered and slow for focused workflows.
  • Approach: prioritize speed, editability, and visual comfort.
  • Solution: ShortcutHub with appbar actions, settings tabs, searchable groups, and background media controls.
